# XerahS Mobile (Android)

XerahS Mobile lets you share files from your phone and upload them quickly.

- Requires Android 8.0 or newer.

## What You Can Do

- Share photos, videos, and files to XerahS from other apps.
- Upload one file or many files in the same share.
- See upload progress and results in the app.
- Copy uploaded links to your clipboard.
- Copy error messages when an upload fails.
- Choose where uploads go, including Amazon S3 and custom uploaders.
- Automatically convert HEIC/HEIF photos to PNG before upload (optional).
- View upload history.
- Search, copy, delete, or clear history entries.

## How To Use

1. Open XerahS and set up an upload destination in **Settings**.
2. In Photos, Files, or another app, tap **Share** and choose **XerahS**.
3. XerahS uploads your file(s) and shows the result links.
4. Open **History** any time to find previous uploads.

## Current Limitations

- The main flow is sharing files to XerahS from other apps.
- In-app file picking is not available yet.
